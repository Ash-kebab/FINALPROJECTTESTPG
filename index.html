Welcome to FRC 101

Your comprehensive guide to First Robotics

Getting started with FRC is complicated and sometimes very overwhelming , This site will point you to resources based on which subteam you want to join. You can also take a look at our comprehensive directory of FRC resource links if you just want to browse. WHat you first want to decide is what subteam you will be joining. The options are as follows : Drive (Drives the robot) , Build (Builds the robot) , CAD (3D models and designs the robot) , Design (Provides major ideas for what we want our robot to do) , Controls (Codes , Tests , and runs the robot) , electrical (Wires the robot and makes sure all motors and sensors are working) , Marketing (Fundraises / posts on social media for the team to get us sponsorships) p.s (Keep in mind , if you are on controls , you are automaticly on electrical and if you are on CAD you are automaticly on Design). After you select what subteam you will be joining , click the grid next to the FRC-101 logo and you will see a list of subteams. Click the subteam you want to join and it will show a list of resources to get you started with each subteam. Or you can go to the Library and view each tool and a short descrption from their website , as well as a link either to the download or the sign-up page. Thank you for using FRC 101 !
